AFTER THE FALL

RELEASING JULY 4th

Soft Carnage will be releasing their latest LP "After the Fall" on July 4th 2020. The single giving this LP it's name is sung by female lead singer, Remy Carlisle, deals with a destain for authority and how foolish pride inevitably precedes a fall (credited to Sir John Dalberg-Acton).

The Band

Soft Carnage is an alt-rock band made up of lead singer, Remy Carlisle, guitarist, Don Simone, and drummer, Donald Albright. Heavily influenced by bands like Garbage, and Siouxsie and the Banshees, the band and label feel they (the band) share the typical hyper-political, angry alt rock crowd. They combine haunting melodic vocals with political lyrics.
